§ 3.7

Process Step Card

Three steps, one row: Completá · Comparamos · Elegís. The homepage's "Cómo funciona" anchor. No card chrome — these are visual diagrams more than UI.

3 STEPS · desktop row · dashed connector
01
Completá
Tu auto, tu zona y un par de datos más. Te lleva 2 minutos.
02
Comparamos
Cruzamos las 14 aseguradoras con tu perfil. Sin sesgos comerciales.
03
Elegís
Ves todo lado a lado. Elegís el plan. Firmás digital.
LayoutDesktop: 3 horizontal cards in equal columns · no card background. Mobile: vertical stack · connector removed · steps separated by space-12 (48 px).
Step containerNo background, no border, no shadow — the step is a typographic + iconographic composition, not a UI surface. Width: equal third of container-default. Padding space-6 (24 px) horizontal · 0 vertical. Center-aligned text.
Step number · decorativeFormat: 01 · 02 · 03 (leading zero, hardcoded). Typography: display-2xl (96 px · serif · 400 · −0.03 em). Color: marine-50 (very light). Position: absolute · top-left of the step container · z-index 0 · partially behind the icon. aria-hidden="true". SR-only span: <span class="sr-only">Paso 1 de 3:</span> precedes the step title so screen-readers read "Paso 1 de 3: Completá" — the visual number is decorative ornament.
Icon containerCircle · diameter 64 px · background marine-100 · centered horizontally within the step. z-index 1 so it sits in front of the decorative number. Icon inside: icon-lg (24 px) · color marine-700 · stroke 1.75. Step icons: step 1 file-text, step 2 refresh-cw (two-arrow loop), step 3 check-circle. Top margin space-3 (12) from the decorative number's top (visually overlapping ~50%).
TitleTypography h4 (22 · 600 · sans) · color sand-900 · top margin space-4 (16) from icon container.
DescriptionTypography body-m (16 / 26 · 400) · color sand-600 · max-width 240 px · center-aligned · top margin space-2 (8).
Connector (desktop only)Style: 1.5 px dashed line (dash pattern 6 4) · color marine-200. Position: from step 1's icon-container right edge to step 2's icon-container left edge (and the same between steps 2 and 3). Vertical: aligned with the icon container's vertical center. Terminator: the line ends in a chevron-right icon (icon-xs · color marine-200 · 4 px before the next icon container). Implementation: rendered as an absolutely-positioned pseudo-element of the parent step row, not as a per-card border. Hidden below md entirely.
AccessibilityThe row is an <ol> · each step is an <li>. Step title is <h3> within the surrounding section. SR-only "Paso N de 3:" prefix supplies the step number explicitly.
DoKeep the dashed connector exclusively decorative and desktop-only. On mobile, removing it tightens the vertical rhythm.
Don'tDon't render the decorative number in marine-700 to "make it pop". The whole point of marine-50 is that it functions as ornament — readable when the eye lingers, invisible when it scans.
