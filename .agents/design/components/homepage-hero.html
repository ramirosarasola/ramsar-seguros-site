§ 2.1

Homepage Hero

The single highest-value surface on the site. Job-to-be-done: in one viewport, deliver the SEO keyword ("seguros de auto Argentina") inside an authoritative H1 and route the visitor to the cotizador without scrolling.

DESKTOP · TEXT-LEFT (55) + VISUAL-RIGHT (45)

Tu auto, asegurado en 2 minutos.

Compará pólizas de 14 aseguradoras del país y elegí la cobertura que mejor se ajusta a tu auto y a tu bolsillo.

✓ Gratis · sin compromiso ✓ En menos de 2 minutos ✓ 14 aseguradoras comparadas
Illustration · marine + amber
ContainerOuter: full-bleed background (paper, no rounded corners, no max-width on the color band). Inner: container-default (1200 px max · 64 / 24 px outer padding). The hero's color is full-bleed; the hero's content is contained.
HeightDesktop: min-height: 640 px (content-driven). Targets one viewport on a 1366×768 laptop with global nav above (72 px) — leaves the insurer-strip visible without scroll. Tablet (md): 560 px. Mobile (xs/sm): auto height, content-driven, ~480 px typical.
Vertical paddingDesktop: space-24 (96 px) top · space-20 (80 px) bottom. Mobile: space-16 (64) · space-12 (48).
LayoutOption A — Text 55% / Visual 45% — chosen. Justification: (1) The H1 wraps to a clean 2–3 lines at 55% column width with our Display-XL leading, which is essential for SEO crawlers reading line one and humans reading the promise. (2) A centred text + bg-visual hero (Option B) would force the H1 to compete with the illustration's chroma and reduce ranking-text legibility. (3) The 45% visual slot accommodates a real illustration without imagery feeling decorative — it earns its place by anchoring the page brand visually right where the eye scans next after the H1.
Grid2-column · template 1.22fr 1fr (≈55/45) · column gap space-12 (48 px) · align-items center.
Background · colorSolid bg-page (sand-50, #F8F7F4). No gradient. The page is cream-on-cream; the hero is unified with the rest of the document and the visual + amber accents do the chromatic work.
Background · textureOne decorative element: a 1 px marine-200 @ 28% α hairline rule beneath the trust-row, extending the full container width. Reads as "specification line", not "web decoration". Nothing else — no gradient mesh, no noise, no shapes.
Visual · styleIllustration — chosen over photography. Why: (a) Stock photography of Argentine drivers reads templated; original photoshoots cost five figures and freeze the brand into one demographic. (b) Illustration is recolorable via tokens — when palette shifts, the visual ships with it. (c) Illustration carries the "smart friend" tone without the parasocial weight of a photographed face.
Visual · subject"The garaged car at dusk." A 3/4 front view of a generic compact sedan parked under a soft amber pool of light. Mid-flat illustration style — geometric shapes with restrained gradient shading, no outlines, no faces, no logos. The car body fills 65% of the visual frame, sits on a horizontal ground-line, with a single Eclipse-mark glyph orbiting above the roof at 1/8 scale to read as "covered" — symbolic, not literal. Surroundings: implied garage — two vertical rule-lines marking columns at left and right edges, a flat horizon plate.
Visual · color allocationCar body: marine-500. Underlight pool: radial gradient amber-300 → amber-500 @ 0% α. Ground line: sand-300. Eclipse glyph: amber-500 ring, sand-50 core. Highlights/reflections: marine-300. No black ink anywhere in the illustration — it lives in the document's palette.
Visual · dimensionsAspect ratio 5 : 4 · max-width 540 px. SVG, optimized. Rendered at 100% no scaling.
H1 · copy"Tu auto, asegurado en 2 minutos." The italic "2 minutos" carries the value prop; the rest delivers the SEO keyword stem "asegurado" (matches "seguros de auto"). H1 is rendered as HTML text (never image-text) per SEO §06.
H1 · typographydisplay-xl (72 / 76 · −0.025 em · serif · 400) · color text-brand (marine-700) · italic span "2 minutos" uses mark color (#C14A1F). Max-width 12 ch (forces a 2-line wrap that the eye scans cleanly).
H1 · bottom marginspace-5 (20 px).
Subheadline · copy"Compará pólizas de 14 aseguradoras del país y elegí la cobertura que mejor se ajusta a tu auto y a tu bolsillo." Spanish-AR voseo ("elegí"). Lengths under 160 chars (works as meta description fallback).
Subheadline · typographybody-xl (20 / 32 · 400) · color text-secondary (sand-700). Max-width 42 ch.
Subheadline · bottom marginspace-8 (32 px) before the CTA group.
CTA · primaryLabel "Cotizá gratis ahora" trailing arrow-right icon (icon-sm, gap 8 px). Variant btn-primary-xl (height 56 px · padding 16 + 24 · radius-sm). Background marine-700 · text sand-0. Shadow elevation-1 default → elevation-2 on hover with translateY(−1 px).
CTA · secondaryLabel "Ver coberturas". Variant btn-outline-xl (same height 56 px · same radius · 1.5 px marine-700 border · transparent fill · marine-700 label). Hover: fill marine-50.
CTA · gap & arrangementDesktop: horizontal · space-3 (12 px) gap. Mobile: stacked · same gap · both buttons stretch to full container width (flex-direction: column).
CTA · pulseNone. A pulsing CTA in a high-trust insurance context reads as desperate and undermines the brand's "calm advisor" tone. The amber accent appears in trust-row checks and the eclipse glyph — that's enough chromatic pull.
Trust micro-rowSits space-6 (24 px) below CTAs. Three items, each: check icon (icon-xs · marine-700 · solid stroke) + label. Typography label-m (13 · 500) · color sand-700. Items separated by an 11 px wide · 1 px tall · sand-300 vertical rule (NOT a bullet character); rule-gap space-3 (12 px) on each side.
Items:
  • "Gratis · sin compromiso"
  • "En menos de 2 minutos"
  • "14 aseguradoras comparadas"
Insurer stripSits at the bottom of the hero band, full container width. Label above: overline (11 · mono · +0.16 em · uppercase) color sand-500, copy "Comparamos las mejores aseguradoras del país". Bottom margin space-4 (16 px) to logo row.
Logo row: 10 partner marks · grayscale (filter: grayscale(1) · opacity 0.55) · uniform height 28 px · gap space-10 (40 px). Hover an individual mark → opacity 1 · grayscale(0), duration-fast · ease-out.
Mobile overflow: single horizontal row, scrollable, snap-to-item. Edge fade: 24 px linear-gradient(to right, paper, paper @ 0%) mask on both edges to hint at off-screen content. Top margin from trust-row: space-12 (48 px).
Responsive · md (768–1279)Layout becomes 1 column. Visual moves below text, scales to max-width 380 px, centered. CTAs stay horizontal at 768+. Trust-row keeps three items but reduces vertical rule height. Insurer strip stays.
Responsive · xs/sm (< 768)Visual is visible (do not hide) — but reduced to a 240 px max-width centered crop. The illustration carries brand recall that homepage text alone can't deliver. CTAs stack vertically full-width. Trust-row collapses to a 2-column 1 ch grid (item 3 wraps to its own row). Insurer strip becomes horizontal-scroll with edge fade.
AccessibilityH1 is the page's only <h1>. Subhead is <p> (not <h2>). Visual is an <img> or <svg role="img"> with aria-label="Auto compacto en garaje con halo de cobertura — ilustración de marca". The trust-row rule separators are CSS pseudo-elements so screen readers read the three items as a clean list.
PerformanceThe illustration is the LCP element. Inline-SVG or preloaded WebP; total weight ≤ 14 KB. fetchpriority="high". Heading text never appears inside the image asset.
DoRender the H1 as live HTML text and place the keyword inside it. Cotizador conversion follows ranking; ranking follows crawlable H1 text.
Don'tDon't replace the illustration with a smiling-couple stock photo. That look anchored Argentine fintech in 2018 and now reads as cheap; the brand-owned illustration is what makes the hero feel like Ramsar's hero, not anyone's.
