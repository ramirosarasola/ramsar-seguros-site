Grid context3-column desktop · 2-column tablet · 1-column mobile. Column width at lg: 368 px. Column gap space-8 (32 px).
Card dimensionsMin-height 340 px (locks rhythm in mixed-content rows). Padding space-5 (20 px) all sides. Inner stack gap space-3 (12 px).
SurfaceBackground bg-card (sand-0). Border 1 px border-default (sand-300). Radius radius-md (12 px). Shadow elevation-0 default · elevation-2 hover.
"Más popular" badgePosition: absolute · top: −10 px · right: 12 px (floats above the card edge to feel pinned, not contained). Background amber-500 · text midnight-700. Typography label-s (11.5 · 500 · +0.01 em). Padding 3 px / 10 px. Radius radius-full. Icon star (icon-xs, midnight-700) preceding label "Más popular". Rule: exactly one card per grid carries this badge. If the system can't decide, none do.
Logo containerWidth 100% of card content · height 44 px · background sand-100 · radius radius-sm (8 px) · no border. Image fit contain (never cover; insurer marks lose meaning when cropped). Centered both axes. The container always uses sand-100 even if the card is on a tinted background — neutral pad keeps every insurer logo legible.
Company nameTypography h5 (18 / 26 · 600 · sans) · color sand-900 · top margin space-3 (12 px) from logo. The name is rendered as an <a> linking to the insurer landing page (e.g. /aseguradoras/sancor-seguros) — provides a second navigational entry without the whole card being a link. Underline appears on hover, 1 px offset 3 px.
Star rating · starsStar icon size 14 px · filled color amber-500 · empty color sand-300 · half star uses CSS clip-path on a duplicated star at 50% width (no half-star asset needed). Gap between stars 2 px.
Star rating · numbersRating number ("4.4") · typography label-m (13 · 500 · mono · tabular-nums) · color sand-800 · 8 px gap from last star. Review count ("(312)" — no word, just the count in parens) · typography label-m mono · color sand-500 · 6 px gap from rating number.
Coverage bullets3 items, vertical stack, gap space-1 (4 px). Each item: check icon (icon-xs · marine-700 · stroke 1.75) + label. Label typography body-s (14 · 400) · color sand-700 · max one line · truncate with ellipsis. Icon color rationale: marine-700, not success-default — these are feature inclusions, not validation states. Reserving success-green for validated states keeps the semantic palette honest.
Price blockSits above CTA, space-4 (16 px) top margin. Single line, baseline-aligned. "Desde" · typography caption (12 · 400 · +0.005 em · sans) · color sand-500. Price value "$34.800" · typography h4 sans (22 · 600) with font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums · color sand-900 · 4 px gap from "Desde". Suffix "/mes" · typography body-s mono · color sand-500 · 2 px gap from price. Note: prices use Argentine thousands separator (period: 34.800) not comma — locale critical.
CTA buttonLabel "Ver coberturas". Variant btn-primary-md · full card width · top margin space-4 (16 px). Why primary over secondary: on this card, the CTA is the page's call to compare. A grid of secondary buttons reads as "browse"; a grid of primary buttons reads as "choose" — which matches user intent on a comparison page. The competing-primaries risk is mitigated by uniform color (no card "wins" chromatically); the Amber badge is what surfaces the one recommendation.
States · hoverBorder color → marine-300, shadow → elevation-2, translateY(−2 px), CTA bg darkens marine-700 → marine-800. All interpolated together at duration-default (220 ms) · ease-out.
States · focus-visibleCard-level focus ring (when a keyboard user lands on the card via Tab on the CTA): 2 px marine-700 outline with 4 px marine-700 @ 18% halo, 2 px offset. Applied to the CTA, not the card itself — the card is not a link.
ResponsiveAt md: 2-column grid. At xs/sm: 1-column, full width. Card internals unchanged. CTA remains full-width.
AccessibilityCard is a <article>; it is not a single link. The company name and CTA are independent <a> elements. aria-labelledby points to the H5. Star rating row: role="img" aria-label="Calificación: 4.4 de 5 estrellas, 312 reseñas". The bullets list is a real <ul>.
DoMake the company name a real link to the insurer page. Some users want depth before they want a quote; deliver both entries within one card.
Don'tDon't wrap the entire card in a single <a>. It collapses the two destinations (insurer page · quote flow) into one and breaks the per-element analytics that drives the recommendation badge.
