§ 3.6

Testimonial Card

Quote-driven social proof from real customers. Appears in the homepage "Lo que dicen" section and on insurer pages as part of the rating block.

TESTIMONIAL · 4-line clamp · quote-mark decorative
"
★ ★ ★ ★ ★
Cotizé un sábado a la noche y el lunes ya tenía la póliza. La diferencia con lo que pagaba antes fue de casi $9.000 al mes.
Federico R.
Toyota Corolla 2022 · Rosario
CardBackground sand-0. Border 1 px sand-100 (deliberately softer than §3.1 to differentiate testimonial cards from product cards). Radius radius-lg (16 px). Shadow elevation-1. Padding space-6 (24 px) all sides.
Decorative quote markCharacter " (opening double-curly · U+201C). Typography display-2xl (96 px · serif · 400). Color amber-300. Position: top-left of card, partially overlapping the top edge — sits with negative top margin −8 px and a line-height of 0 to anchor only the baseline. aria-hidden="true". Behind the rating row (z-index 0; rating is z-1).
Star ratingSame star spec as §3.1 — 14 px stars, amber-500 filled / sand-300 empty. Number adjacent to stars omitted on testimonial card (the count is in the author meta below). Top margin from decorative quote: space-3 (12 px).
Quote textTypography body-l (18 / 30 · 400) · serif italic not applied (Geist italic doesn't exist in our system — see Typography rules §03). Instead: regular weight 400 + the surrounding curly quotes carry the "voice" affordance. Color sand-800. Max 4 lines · ellipsis. Top margin space-3 (12 px) from stars.
Author rowFlex row · top border 1 px sand-200 · top padding space-4 (16 px) · top margin space-5 (20 px) from quote.
Avatar40 px circle · 1 px sand-200 border · object-fit cover. Initials fallback: marine-50 bg + marine-700 serif italic.
Author nameFirst name + last initial only ("Federico R."). Typography label-m (13 · 600 · sans) · color sand-900. Privacy rule: never full surname.
Vehicle & locationFormat: "Toyota Corolla 2022 · Rosario". Typography body-xs mono (13 · 400 · +0.005 em) · color sand-500. Top margin from name: 2 px.
Grid layoutDesktop ≥ lg: 3 columns · gap space-6 (24 px). md: 2 columns. xs/sm: horizontal-scroll carousel — single card per viewport, snap-to-item, 24 px peek of the next card on the right to hint at overflow. Edge-fade mask on the right edge.
Accessibility<blockquote> wrapping the quote text + <cite> wrapping the author row. Decorative quote mark is aria-hidden. Star rating: role="img" aria-label="5 de 5 estrellas".
DoInclude the vehicle model in the author meta. It is the single fact that converts "stranger's opinion" into "person like me".
Don'tDon't put the testimonial card on a tinted brand background. Sand-0 on cream is what makes the quote feel like paper; tinting flattens it into UI.
