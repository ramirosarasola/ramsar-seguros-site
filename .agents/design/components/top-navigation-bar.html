The desktop primary nav. Three jobs: hold the brand mark, route to high-equity SEO pages, and surface the single "Cotizá ahora" conversion target at all times.

Layout (L → R)Logo · gap-20 (80 px) · nav-link cluster · gap-12 (48 px) · Primary CTA. Logo is left-aligned to container-default; CTA is right-aligned to the same container.
Height72 px default · 64 px scrolled. Vertical padding space-4 (16) default, space-3 (12) scrolled. Total inner band is 40 px at rest.
LogoEclipse wordmark, height 28 px default → 24 px scrolled. Clickable hit-zone padded to 44×44 px. Always links to /; never visited-state colored.
Nav links · countMaximum 5, recommended 3. Current set: Seguros (dropdown · 14 aseguradoras), Vehículos (dropdown · 15 marcas), Blog. Adding a 4th link requires dropping the dropdown indicator from another to keep visual rhythm.
Link typographylabel-l (15 / 20 · 500 · sans). Tracking 0. No transform.
Link gapspace-8 (32 px) between adjacent links. Last link → CTA: space-12 (48 px) to visually separate destinations from conversion.
Link color · defaultLabel text-secondary (sand-700). Dropdown chevron: icon-xs, sand-500, gap 4 px after label.
Link color · hoverLabel → text-brand (marine-700). Chevron → marine-700. duration-fast (120) · ease-out.
Active / currentUnderline indicator. 2 px solid marine-700, full label width, sits 12 px below baseline (visually centered in the band's bottom half). Animates in via scaleX(0)→scaleX(1) from center, duration-default · ease-out. No background pill, no left border — those treatments belong to vertical nav.
Dropdown triggerLinks with sub-pages carry a chevron-down icon (icon-xs) trailing the label. On hover/focus the chevron rotates 180° in duration-default · ease-out. Hover delay 120 ms before opening to prevent accidental triggers; close delay 240 ms on pointer-leave to forgive cursor overshoot.
Primary CTALabel "Cotizá ahora" (Spanish-AR voseo). Variant btn-primary-md (40 px tall, 12+16 padding). Right-aligned. Carries no icon by default; on viewports ≥ xl add a trailing arrow-right (icon-sm, gap 6 px) for affordance.
Sticky behaviorYes, sticky. The CTA must be reachable at all times — a Ramsar visitor's most likely intent on any page is "I want a quote." position: sticky; top: 0.
Scroll transitionTriggered at scrollY ≥ 24 px. Height 72→64, logo 28→24, vertical padding 16→12, background opacity 100→96%. All four interpolate together over duration-default (220 ms) · ease-out. Reverses at scrollY ≤ 8 px (deadband prevents thrash).
Background · restSolid bg-page (sand-50). Never transparent over hero — Ramsar's hero is cream too, and a transparent nav would float without an anchor.
Background · scrolledbg-page at 96% α with backdrop-filter: blur(12px). The 4% transparency lets content texture show through and signals layered chrome.
Border / shadowRest: border-bottom: 1px solid border-subtle. Scrolled: border drops, elevation-2 shadow appears. Both interpolate alongside the height transition.
Z-indexz-50 = 50. Below modals (100), above page content (auto) and dropdown panels (40).
ResponsiveVisible at md (≥ 768 px) and above. Below md: replaced by §1.2 Mobile Navigation Bar.
Accessibility<header> wrapping <nav aria-label="Principal">. Links are <a>, not buttons. Skip-link "Saltar al contenido" appears at top on focus (positions absolute, transforms in). Focus ring per §05.
KeyboardTab order: skip-link → logo → each link L→R → CTA. Enter/Space activate. aria-current="page" on the active link.
DoKeep the link cluster < 5 items. Conversion suffers measurably past 5; a missed visitor never opens a dropdown.
Don'tDon't recolor the CTA to amber here. The CTA in nav must read as "primary action of the site"; amber is for in-page accents and "Recommended" affordances only.
