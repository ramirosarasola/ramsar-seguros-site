§ 2.3

Blog Article Hero

The reading entrance. Establishes article topic, credibility, and visual context, then hands the reader to body copy. No CTA — the visitor is in read mode; a CTA here interrupts the contract.

Containercontainer-narrow (720 px max · 48 / 20 px outer padding) — same reading column the article body uses. Hero and body share gutters; the rhythm of the article is unbroken.
HeightContent-driven. No min-height enforced. Top padding space-12 (48 px), bottom padding space-8 (32 px) before article body.
Elements (top → bottom)
  1. Breadcrumb (§1.5) · left-aligned · ends in category name as last link before current
  2. Category badge · left-aligned · bottom margin space-3 (12)
  3. H1 · left-aligned · bottom margin space-5 (20)
  4. Metadata bar · bottom margin space-6 (24)
  5. Hero image · bottom margin space-4 (16) to caption
  6. Caption (optional) · bottom margin space-12 (48) to article body
Category badge · colorsEach of 5 categories carries a fixed color treatment (token-driven, recolorable later):
  • Consejos — bg marine-50 · text marine-700
  • Comparativas — bg amber-300 · text amber-700
  • Novedades — bg info-light (#E1ECF7) · text info-dark (#173A66)
  • Guías — bg success-light (#E4F1EA) · text success-dark (#0F4A2C)
  • Opiniones — bg sand-100 · text sand-800
Badge variant badge-md · radius-full · padding 4 px / 10 px. Same color appears on the matching blog-card category badge for visual continuity.
H1 · typographyh1 (44 / 50 · −0.018 em · serif · 400) on desktop. On mobile clamp to display-l → 32 / 38. Color sand-900. Max 60 characters — enforced — for SEO title-tag fallback and clean wrap at 720 px. If editor exceeds: H1 truncates display only; full title remains in <title> and OG tags.
Metadata barSingle row, baseline-aligned. Order: avatar · author name · separator · publish date · separator · read time · separator · "Actualizado" date (optional).

Author avatar: circular 32 px · 1 px sand-200 border. Fallback: initials in marine-50 bg + marine-700 text · serif initials.

Author name: label-m (13 · 500 · sans) · color sand-800 · linked to author profile (/blog/autor/[slug]).

Separator dot: CSS pseudo-element "·" · color sand-300 · space-2 padding each side.

Publish date: label-m · color sand-600. Format Spanish-AR: "12 may 2026". Marked-up with <time datetime="2026-05-12">.

Read time: label-m · color sand-600. Format: "8 min de lectura" (full label first article, abbreviated "8 min" after first separator if the bar would wrap on mobile).

Updated date: caption (12 · 400 · +0.005 em) · color sand-500. Format: "(Actualizado: 14 may 2026)". Only present if updated-date differs from publish-date by ≥ 30 days. Marked-up with <time datetime="..."> — SEO-significant for evergreen content.

Hero image · dimensionsAspect ratio fixed 16 : 9. Width 100% of reading column (max 720 px). Border radius radius-md (12 px) all four corners. Object-fit cover.
Hero image · captionTypography caption (12 · 400 · +0.005 em) · color sand-500. Left-aligned, matching the rest of the article body's left-aligned text. Wrapped in <figcaption> inside a parent <figure>. Optional — omit the element entirely if no caption (do not render an empty space).
Hero image · alt & SEOEvery hero image carries alt describing its content factually — not the article title. Example: alt="Tablero comparativo de cobertura entre Sancor y Zurich con valores de prima anual." Length 60–125 chars. Files named by topic ("comparativa-sancor-zurich-2026.webp"), never "hero.jpg". Width 1600 px source, served via responsive srcset at 1×, 1.5×, 2×.
No CTA · justificationThe mid-article CTA block (§4.7) appears at the 50% scroll mark, where the reader has signalled engagement. Placing a CTA above the fold would interrupt the reading contract and measurably reduce article completion rates. The conversion offer is honoured later, when it's earned.
ResponsiveLayout unchanged structurally. H1 clamps from 44 → 32 px between md and xs. Metadata bar wraps to 2 rows when needed: row 1 = avatar + author + date; row 2 = read time + updated date. Hero image keeps 16:9 aspect.
AccessibilitySingle <h1>. Author avatar carries empty alt (decorative) when accompanied by author name. Hero image is the <img> inside <figure> with <figcaption>. Dates use <time>.
DoSurface the "Actualizado" date when content is materially refreshed. Freshness is a documented ranking signal for evergreen finance content.
Don'tDon't drop a "Cotizá ahora" CTA into this hero. Reading-mode interruptions cost completion and trust; the mid-article block converts better.
