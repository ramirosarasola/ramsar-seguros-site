Mobile · placementInline, above the article body, below the hero. Width 100% of container-narrow.
Mobile · default stateCollapsed. Single-row trigger: label "Ver contenido del artículo (8 secciones)" · variant btn-ghost-md · full-width · trailing chevron-down icon. Bottom border 1 px sand-200.
Mobile · expandedReveals the same items as desktop, full-width, indented. Expand animation: grid-template-rows 0fr → 1fr · duration-slow (360 ms) · ease-in-out. Chevron rotates 180°. No sticky behavior on mobile (the floor scrolls with the page).
AccessibilityWrapped in <nav aria-label="Contenido del artículo">. Items are a <ol> with smooth-scroll anchor links. Active item carries aria-current="true". Mobile trigger is a real <button aria-expanded>.
DoMake active-state tracking based on the heading nearest to the top of the viewport. Updating on visual center causes the active item to lag the user's perception of "where I am".
Don'tDon't try to make the TOC sticky on mobile. There's no horizontal room, and a sticky bar above body content blocks the reading column at exactly the moment the user wants the reading column unobstructed.
