§ 4.5

Author Bio Card

Sits at the foot of every blog article. Establishes author credibility (E-E-A-T signal for Google) and gives readers a face — a small but consequential trust transfer in a financial-content context.

AUTHOR BIO · top accent rule · 72 px avatar
Lucía Andrade
Especialista en seguros · Ramsar Editorial
12 años cubriendo el mercado asegurador argentino. Antes en La Nación Economía. Vive en Rosario, maneja un Corolla.
ContainerBackground sand-50 · border 1 px sand-200 · top border 4 px marine-500 (the only visual accent the component carries). Radius radius-lg (16 px). Padding space-6 (24 px) all sides. Width: 100% of container-narrow.
Layout · desktopHorizontal flex · avatar left · content right · gap space-5 (20 px) · align-items flex-start.
Layout · mobileStacks vertically · avatar centered above content · gap space-4 (16 px). Content text remains left-aligned for readability.
Avatar · desktopCircle · 72 px diameter · 3 px sand-0 border · elevation-1 shadow. Object-fit cover. Fallback (no image): initials in serif italic, marine-700 on marine-50, same dimensions.
Avatar · mobileDiameter reduces to 56 px · border 2 px · same elevation.
Author nameTypography h5 sans (18 · 600) on desktop — actually we use serif here for tone: 20 · serif · 400 · color sand-900. Bottom margin 2 px (snug to role).
RoleFormat: "[Role] · Ramsar Editorial". Typography label-m (13 · 500 · sans) · color marine-600. Bottom margin space-2 (8 px).
BioTypography body-s (14 / 22 · 400) · color sand-600. Maximum 180 characters — enforced. Truncate-with-link on overflow ("…leé más") routes to author profile.
Social rowTop margin space-3 (12 px) from bio. Platforms: LinkedIn and X / Twitter only (the two platforms where AR financial-journalism authority lives). Icon size icon-sm (16 px) · color sand-400 rest · sand-700 hover. Gap space-3 (12 px) between icons. Hit target 32 × 32 px transparent padding. rel="me noopener" on links.
Placement on pageAt end of article body · top margin space-12 (48 px) from last paragraph. Sits above the "Related articles" rail. One per article only.
AccessibilityAuthor name is wrapped in an <a> to /blog/autor/[slug]. Avatar carries alt="Foto de [name]" if real image; empty alt if initial-fallback (decorative). Social links use aria-label="Perfil de [name] en LinkedIn".
SEO · E-E-A-TLinked to Person JSON-LD schema with author name, role, image URL, and sameAs (social links). On financial-content topics, named-author bylines with linked profiles measurably improve YMYL rankings.
DoUse a serif for the author name even though most card titles are sans. The serif signals "byline" — the same convention every newspaper uses — and the convention is the credibility.
Don'tDon't add a "Síganos" or "Suscribite" CTA inside this card. The author bio earns trust precisely by not selling at the moment of credit-claim.
