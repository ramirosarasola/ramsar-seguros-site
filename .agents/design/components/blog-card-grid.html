Card structureImage (flush top) + content area. Border 1 px sand-200. Radius radius-md (12 px) — applied to outer card; image inherits top corners. Background sand-0. Shadow elevation-0 default · elevation-1 hover. overflow: hidden so the image hover-zoom is clipped.
ImageAspect ratio fixed 16 : 9. Width 100% of card. Border radius inherits top-left + top-right (radius-md); bottom corners 0. Object-fit cover. Hover: scale(1.03) over duration-fast (120 ms) · ease-out · origin center.
Category badge · positionAbsolute · bottom: 12 px · left: 12 px overlaid on image bottom-left.
Category badge · styleSolid background (not semi-transparent — translucent reads as overlay UI, solid reads as label). Radius radius-full. Padding 3 px (vertical) / 10 px (horizontal). Typography label-s (11.5 · 600 · sans · +0.02 em).
Category · 6 fixed colors
  • Consejos · bg marine-50 · text marine-700
  • Comparativas · bg amber-300 · text amber-700
  • Novedades · bg info-light (#E1ECF7) · text info-dark (#173A66)
  • Guías · bg success-default (#1F7A4D) · text sand-0
  • Por marca · bg sand-200 · text sand-800
  • Por aseguradora · bg marine-700 · text sand-0
The same 6 colors appear on the blog-article hero badge (§2.3) — continuity is the whole point.
Content paddingspace-5 (20 px) all sides. Internal vertical gap space-2 (8 px) between blocks (headline · excerpt · meta).
HeadlineTypography h5 (18 / 26 · 600 · sans) · color sand-900. Max 2 lines · ellipsis (-webkit-line-clamp: 2). Bottom margin space-2 (8 px).
ExcerptTypography body-s (14 / 22 · 400) · color sand-600. Max 3 lines · ellipsis. Bottom margin space-3 (12 px).
Metadata rowSingle line · baseline-aligned. Order: avatar · author · separator · date · separator · read-time.
Avatar: 24 px circle · 1 px sand-200 border. Fallback initials in marine-50 + marine-700 italic serif.
Author / date / read-time typography label-s mono (11.5 · 500). Colors: author sand-700 · date sand-500 · read-time sand-500.
Separators are "·" CSS pseudo-elements at sand-300, 6 px padding each side.
Gap avatar → author: space-2 (8 px).
States · defaultAs described above.
States · hoverShadow elevation-0 → elevation-1. Image scales 1.03. Headline color shifts sand-900 → marine-600. All over 120 ms · ease-out.
States · focus-visibleCard receives standard focus ring (the whole card is a single link).
Responsive3 / 2 / 1 columns across lg / md / xs. Image aspect stays 16:9 at every breakpoint. Headline max-lines stays 2; excerpt drops from 3 → 2 below sm.
AccessibilityEntire card is one <a>. aria-label assembled at render: "[Headline] — [Categoría] — [Read time] de lectura — Publicado [Date]". Avatar carries empty alt. Title is the linked text; ARIA label augments, never replaces.
DoKeep category colors stable across hero, badge, and card. The color is the category — change it and the system goes blind.
Don'tDon't add a "Leer más →" link inside the card body. The whole card is the link; a second affordance halves the click target.
Figma propscategory: 6 enum — image: instance-swap — avatar: instance-swap — text overrides for headline · excerpt · author · date · read-time.
