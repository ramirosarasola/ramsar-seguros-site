§ 4.7

In-Article CTA Block

A conversion prompt embedded inside the article body. Earned, not imposed — appears only after the reader has invested in the content. Two instances per article maximum.

MID-ARTICLE CTA · marine surface · amber action
¿Querés saber cuánto pagarías?

Cotizá en 2 minutos y comparamos por vos. Sin tarjeta, sin compromiso.

Compará en menos tiempo del que leíste esto.
14 aseguradoras · 1 cotizador · 2 minutos.
Two visual variantsA · Solid Marine (mid-article). Full-saturation surface, amber CTA. Used at the 50% scroll mark. B · Marine-tint with accent rule (in-flow). Quiet treatment used at the end of the article, above the author bio. Variant A interrupts; Variant B closes.
Width100% of reading column (container-narrow). Never breaks out of the column.
Variant A · surfaceBackground marine-700 · text sand-0. Radius radius-lg (16 px). Padding space-6 (24 px) all sides. Decorative element: an amber-300 @ 18% α circle (140 px) positioned absolute · top: −40 px · right: −40 px · overflow hidden on container. Reads as "warmth bleeding off the edge".
Variant A · headlineTypography h4 serif (24 · 400) · color sand-0. Example: "¿Querés saber cuánto pagarías?" Bottom margin space-2 (8 px).
Variant A · subtextTypography body-s (14 · 400) · color sand-0 @ 85% α · max-width 36 ch. Example: "Cotizá en 2 minutos y comparamos por vos. Sin tarjeta, sin compromiso."
Variant A · CTAVariant btn-primary-md · amber colorway: background amber-500 · text midnight-700. Hover: amber-600. Label "Cotizá ahora gratis" + arrow-right icon-sm trailing.
Variant B · surfaceBackground marine-50. Left border 4 px marine-500 (the accent treatment that visually attaches it to article reading flow, like a pull-quote rule). No top/right/bottom borders. Radius radius-sm (8 px) on the right edge corners only; left corners 0. Padding space-5 (20 px).
Variant B · headlineTypography h5 serif (20 · 400) · color sand-900. Bottom margin 4 px.
Variant B · subtextTypography body-s (14 · 400) · color sand-700.
Variant B · CTAVariant btn-primary-md (standard marine). Inline with text on desktop (right-aligned via grid); stacks below on mobile.
LayoutDesktop: 2-column grid · text-left + CTA-right · column gap space-5 (20 px) · align-items center. Mobile: stacks · CTA full-width below text · gap space-4 (16 px).
Placement ruleMid-article (variant A): after the 3rd or 4th paragraph of the article body — never in the first two paragraphs (the reader hasn't paid in yet, and a CTA before they've extracted value reads as a bait-and-switch). End-of-article (variant B): above the §4.5 Author Bio, after the last content paragraph. Bottom margin to following element: space-12 (48 px).
Frequency ruleMaximum two instances per article — one of each variant. A third instance trains readers to skim past them.
AccessibilityThe block is a <aside aria-label="Cotizá tu seguro">. The headline is an <h3> within the article hierarchy. Decorative amber circle on variant A is CSS only (zero DOM impact).
DoUse variant A once in the body, variant B once at the end. Their visual contrast prevents the second CTA from feeling like a repeat of the first.
Don'tDon't place either variant in the first two paragraphs. Conversion-block-before-payoff reads as a paywall — the worst possible signal in a free-content brand.
Figma propsvariant: A-solid · B-tint — showSubtext: boolean — text overrides for headline · subtext · CTA label.
