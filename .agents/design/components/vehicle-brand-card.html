Shape & sizeSquare. Desktop 176 × 176 px at lg. Tablet 152 × 152 px. Mobile 112 × 112 px. Padding space-4 (16 px) all sides at every size.
SurfaceBackground sand-50. Border 1 px border-default (sand-200 · slightly softer than insurer cards). Radius radius-lg (16 px). Shadow elevation-0.
Logo containerCentered circle. Diameter 64 px desktop · 56 px tablet · 40 px mobile. Background always white (sand-0) regardless of card hover state — protects logo legibility. Border-radius 50%. Internal padding space-2 (8 px). Image fit contain. The container reaches white even on hover; only the card body shifts.
Brand nameTypography label-l (15 · 500 · sans) · color sand-900 · center-aligned · top margin space-3 (12 px) from logo container.
Model countText "X modelos" · typography caption (12 · 400 · +0.005 em) · color sand-500 · center-aligned · top margin 2 px from brand name (snug deliberately — they read as a paired label).
States · defaultAs specified above.
States · hoverBackground marine-50 · border marine-200 · brand name color marine-700. Logo container stays white. Transition duration-default (220) · ease-out. No translate, no shadow change — the chromatic shift carries the entire interaction.
States · focus-visibleStandard focus ring per §05 around the entire card (it is a single link).
Grid layoutDesktop ≥ lg: 6 columns (15 brands across 3 rows · last row half-filled — anchor the 15th to the leftmost column). md: 4 columns. sm: 4 columns smaller cards. xs: 3 columns at 112 px. Column gap space-3 (12 px) · row gap space-3 (12 px).
AccessibilityThe entire card is a single <a> (unlike Insurance card, there is no secondary destination here). aria-label="Ver seguros para [Brand]" — model count is read separately by virtue of being a sibling text node within the link.
DoAlways render the logo container in white, regardless of card background state. Logos are protected — they predate Ramsar.
Don'tDon't recolor the brand name to amber-500 on hover. Amber is reserved for accent/badge use; using it here would dilute the system.
