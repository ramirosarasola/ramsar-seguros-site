Price Range Indicator

Shows minimum · average · maximum monthly insurance cost for a vehicle model. Designed to communicate one number (the average) in under two seconds, while remaining honest about variance.

Container widthFull content column. The component is single-instance per page (only appears on vehicle-model pages).
Section label"Costo estimado del seguro" · typography h6 (16 · 600 · sans) · color sand-700 · bottom margin space-3 (12 px).
TrackHeight 8 px. Border-radius radius-full. Background base color sand-200 (visible only where the gradient hasn't reached on a partial-range component variant — at full range it is entirely covered).
Fill · gradientlinear-gradient(90deg, marine-300 0%, marine-500 50%, marine-700 100%). Saturation/lightness increase toward the right reads as "more coverage / higher commitment" — semantically aligned with price.
Min markerCircle · 12 px diameter · background marine-300 · 2 px sand-0 border. Positioned absolute · left: 0 · vertically centered on track.
Avg markerCircle · 16 px · background marine-600 · 2 px sand-0 border · elevation-1 shadow. Position: left = avg-percentage. Larger + shadowed so the eye lands here first.
Max markerCircle · 12 px · background marine-700 · 2 px sand-0 border. Position: right: 0.
"Promedio" calloutFloating above the avg marker. Background marine-700 · text sand-0. Typography label-s (11.5 · 600 · sans). Padding 3 px / 10 px. Radius radius-sm (8 px). CSS triangle tail below the pill (5 px transparent / 5 px marine-700 border-top) points at the avg marker. Positioned absolute · top above track · transform: translateX(−50%) anchored to avg-percentage.
Labels below trackSingle row, justify-content space-between. Top margin from track: space-4 (16 px).

Min: Typography caption (12 · 400) · text "Desde" color sand-500 · price "$28.500/mes" typography label-s mono (11.5 · 500 · tabular-nums) color sand-800.

Avg: Typography label-s (11.5 · 600 · sans) · text "Promedio" + price both color marine-700. Bolder than min/max — the avg label echoes the callout above.

Max: Same treatment as min · text "Hasta" + price.

Currency formatStrictly "$XX.XXX/mes" — period thousands separator, no space before "/mes", no decimals on integer values, "AR$" prefix only on the cotizador page (here ambient context is sufficient).
AnimationOn viewport entry (IntersectionObserver, threshold 0.6): fill gradient animates from 0% width → 100% width over duration-glacial (600 ms) · ease-out. Markers fade in (opacity 0 → 1) at 60% / 80% / 100% progress (staggered). Callout drops in (translateY(−6 px) → 0 + opacity 0 → 1) at the end · duration-default · ease-out. Reduced-motion: fill renders at 100% immediately, markers and callout appear instantly.
ResponsiveLayout unchanged across breakpoints. Below sm (480 px): label row wraps · min/avg/max stack vertically left-aligned · markers and track stay full-width. Callout above the avg may overlap horizontally — drop the triangle tail and place the callout above the section label on mobile if avg position is < 25% or > 75% of width.
AccessibilityTrack + markers are presentational. Adjacent to the visual: a visually-hidden <dl> with the three values ("Mínimo: $28.500/mes" · "Promedio: $42.800/mes" · "Máximo: $68.200/mes") for screen readers. Whole component carries role="group" aria-label="Costo estimado del seguro de Toyota Corolla 2022".
DoAnchor the "Promedio" callout to the avg marker, not to the center of the bar. The marker may not be centered — variance is real, and the callout should follow the data.
Don'tDon't use comma thousands-separators on prices. AR uses periods; commas are decimal markers. Mixed locale on a money component erodes trust faster than any other UI bug.
Figma propsmin · avg · max: numeric inputs — showCallout: boolean — currency: ARS (default) · USD — text override for the section label.
