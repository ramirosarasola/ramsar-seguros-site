Mobile Drawer Menu

Full-height drawer that holds the complete navigation tree on mobile. Slides in from the right to keep the user's thumb anchor (right hand) close to the interactive surface.

TriggerTap of §1.2 hamburger. Also tap of body skip-target on first focus when a screen reader is detected.
Slide directionFrom the right. Argentina (and 85% of mobile users globally) holds the phone right-handed; sliding from the right keeps the thumb closer to interactive items. The left edge becomes the visual "trailing" edge where the close action lives.
AnimationtranslateX(100%) → translateX(0) · duration-slow (360 ms) · ease-in-out. Backdrop fades in parallel.
Backdropbg-overlay = midnight-900 @ 64% α. backdrop-filter: blur(4px) applied. Tap-to-close: yes (single tap anywhere outside the drawer dismisses).
Drawer width88% viewport width · max 420 px. The remaining 12% lets the user see the backdrop and reinforces the "this is dismissable" affordance.
Drawer height100dvh (dynamic viewport, accounts for mobile browser chrome). Background bg-page.
Internal paddingTop space-6 (24) · sides space-5 (20) · bottom space-8 (32) (extra for one-thumb reach to the CTA).
Internal layout

Top row: Logo (height 24 px, links to home) + Close button (X · icon-md · 44×44 target · right-aligned).

Primary links stacked vertically: typography h5 (18/26 · 600 · sans), color sand-900. Per-item padding space-3 (12) top/bottom. Divider 1 px border-subtle between items. Each link a <a>; primary destinations only (Seguros, Vehículos, Blog).

CTA button: appears after primary links, with space-4 (16 px) separator above. Variant btn-primary-lg · full-width · trailing arrow-right icon.

Secondary links: separated by space-5 (20 px) from CTA. Inline row: "Nosotros", "Contacto", "FAQ". Typography body-s (14/22 · 400), color sand-600, gap space-4 (16 px).

Legal strip: bottom-pinned. "Política de Privacidad" · "Términos". Typography body-xs (13/20), color sand-500. Pipe separator in sand-300.

Social row: immediately above legal strip. Instagram · TikTok · LinkedIn · WhatsApp. Icons icon-md, color sand-700, gap space-4 (16 px). (Four platforms only; the AR audience uses these four; X/Twitter and Facebook excluded by user-data review.)

Scroll behaviorDrawer body scrolls internally when content exceeds viewport. Top row (logo + close) is sticky and gains a 1 px bottom border on internal scroll.
Focus trapYes. On open, focus moves to the close button. Tab cycles within the drawer; Shift+Tab cycles in reverse. Last tabbable wraps to first. Escape closes and restores focus to the hamburger trigger.
Body scroll lockWhile open: overflow: hidden on document.body + position-fixed restore on close to preserve scroll position.
Z-indexDrawer z-60 · backdrop z-55. Both above all page chrome.
Accessibilityrole="dialog" aria-modal="true" aria-labelledby="drawer-title" where drawer-title is the visually-hidden phrase "Menú principal".
DoPin the CTA inside the drawer too. Some users open the drawer searching for it; deliver it where they look.
Don'tDon't slide from the left. Left-side drawers feel "navigation-y" but force right-handed users to thumb-stretch on every tap.
