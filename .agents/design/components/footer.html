BackgroundDark — Midnight 700 (bg-inverse · #0B1F2A). Justification: a footer in cream would float ambiguously and dilute the page's reading rhythm. Midnight signals "end of page", anchors the document, and gives the Amber accent on the trust strip a high-contrast bed.
Top section paddingDesktop: space-20 (80 px) top · space-12 (48 px) bottom. Mobile: space-12 / space-10 (48/40).
Top section gridDesktop ≥ lg: 5 columns · template 1.4fr 1fr 1fr 1fr 1fr · column gap space-8 (32 px). Tablet md: 3 columns · brand col spans 3 in row 1, link cols 1+2 in row 2, 3+4 in row 3. Mobile xs/sm: single column, all collapse to accordion (see below).

Mobile stacking orderBrand column (always expanded) → Seguros accordion → Vehículos → Blog → Empresa → Trust strip → Bottom bar (copyright → legal links row → seals row).
Z-indexNative document order — no stacking context. Footer participates in normal scroll, no fixed/sticky behavior.
AccessibilityWrapped in <footer role="contentinfo">. Column headings are <h2> (semantic — Google uses these as section indicators). Social icons carry aria-label in Spanish-AR. Mobile accordion buttons are real <button> with aria-expanded.
SEOEvery link in the top section is internal and crawlable — no nofollow, no rel="sponsored". Total internal-link surface from footer ≈ 29 (5 col headings as anchors + 29 destinations) on every indexed page; this is the project's largest single source of internal link equity. External social links use rel="me noopener".
DoKeep every internal link in the top section dofollow. Footer links are how Ramsar's deep insurer/vehicle pages reach crawlable depth.
Don'tDon't centre the column headings or links. Left-aligned is non-negotiable — centred footer columns become unscannable past four items.
