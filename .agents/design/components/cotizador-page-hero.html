§ 2.4

Cotizador Page Hero

The most stripped-down hero in the system. Lives above the quote widget on /cotizar. Every element exists to absorb one specific anxiety before the user types their first character.

COTIZADOR · stripped nav + reassurance stack
Ramsar

Vamos a buscarte la mejor cobertura.

Te toma menos de 2 minutos. Sin cargos. Sin tarjeta de crédito.

🔒 SSL Seguro 💸 Sin cargo ⚡ Respuesta inmediata 🏢 +20 aseguradoras
NavigationStripped: only the Ramsar logo, centered. No menu links, no hamburger, no Cotizá CTA (the user is already converting). Pseudo-header height 72 px · 1 px border-subtle bottom · sticky on scroll. Why: in a 2-minute conversion flow, navigation links offer exits, not utility. Their absence is a deliberate constraint that lifts completion rate.
Containercontainer-narrow (720 px max) · content centered · horizontal padding space-6 (24 px) mobile / space-12 (48 px) desktop.
HeightContent-driven. Vertical padding space-16 (64 px) top (below stripped nav) · space-8 (32 px) bottom (above the widget card). Mobile space-10 / space-6.
Elements (top → bottom)
  1. Stripped logo-only nav
  2. Vertical space space-16 (64 px)
  3. Headline (H2, not H1 — the page is functional, not landing)
  4. Bottom margin space-3 (12 px)
  5. One-line reassurance
  6. Bottom margin space-6 (24 px)
  7. Trust badges row
  8. Bottom margin space-8 (32 px) to widget card
Headline · copy"Vamos a buscarte la mejor cobertura." First-person plural ("vamos") plants the brand alongside the user. "Buscarte" is voseo — Spanish-AR consistency. No promises about price (managing expectations), only about commitment.
Headline · typographyh2 (36 / 42 · −0.015 em · serif · 400). Not H1 — this page is conversion utility, not a landing page; the H1 is reserved for SEO landing pages where keyword-in-H1 matters. Color sand-900. Centered. Max-width 18 ch.
Reassurance · copy"Te toma menos de 2 minutos. Sin cargos. Sin tarjeta de crédito." Three commitments, each a measured negation of a specific anxiety: time, money, future surprise.
Reassurance · typographybody-l (18 / 30 · 400) · color text-secondary (sand-700). Centered. Max-width 48 ch.
Trust badges row4 badges, horizontally centered, gap space-6 (24 px). Wraps to a 2×2 grid below sm (480 px). Each badge: icon icon-sm (16 px) · stroke marine-700 + label.
Label typography: label-s (11.5 · 500 · +0.01 em) · color sand-600.
Icon → label gap: space-2 (8 px).
Items + anxiety addressed:
  • lock + "SSL Seguro" — "They're asking for my DNI and license plate; is this safe?"
  • circle-check + "Sin cargo" — "Is there a hidden fee at the end?"
  • clock + "Respuesta inmediata" — "How long is this going to take?"
  • shield-check + "+20 aseguradoras" — "Is this a real comparison or just one company's pitch?"
BackgroundPure white (bg-card · #FFFFFF) — one step lighter than bg-page (cream) used everywhere else. Psychological rationale: cream paper is the brand voice; white is the form. The shift to white signals "you are now in a tool, not on a landing page." It also gives the quote-widget card visual independence — when the widget sits on the same color as the hero, it must work harder to feel like an interactive surface.
ResponsiveSingle column at all breakpoints. Headline reduces from 36 → 28 → 24 px between md/sm/xs. Trust badges wrap from 1×4 → 2×2 below 480 px. Stripped nav height stays 72 px desktop / 56 px mobile.
AccessibilityThe cotizador page does have a hidden visually-clipped <h1> ("Cotizador de seguros de auto") for SEO + SR users, placed before the visible H2. The visible H2 is the conversational entry. Trust badges are aria-hidden icons + visible text (icons are decorative).
SEOThe cotizador page is the lowest-priority SEO surface in the system — its title is "Cotizá tu seguro de auto · Ramsar Seguros" but it is set <meta name="robots" content="index, follow"> only for the entry URL; deep-flow URLs (/cotizar/paso-2 etc.) are noindex to keep the indexable graph clean.
DoKeep the four trust badges fixed and ordered as specified. Each was placed against a specific user anxiety; reordering breaks the choreography of reassurance.
Don'tDon't restore the global nav on this page "for consistency". Conversion pages earn their right to remove links the way exit doors earn their right to be wider than entrances.
