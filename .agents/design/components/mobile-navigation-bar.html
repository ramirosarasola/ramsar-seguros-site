Mobile Navigation Bar

The mobile top bar. Same job as desktop nav, compressed to fit one thumb: brand on the left, drawer trigger on the right, persistent quote affordance.

Height56 px · vertical padding space-3 (12 px). Does not shrink on scroll (mobile space is too constrained for a height transition to be felt).
Backgroundbg-page (sand-50). On scroll ≥ 24 px adds elevation-2; no opacity change.
ShadowRest: border-bottom: 1px solid border-subtle. Scrolled: drops border, gains elevation-2.
LogoLeft-aligned, height 22 px. Padded to a 44×44 hit-target. Tap returns to /.
Hamburger iconLucide menu · size icon-md (20 px) · stroke 1.75 · color sand-800. Hit-target 44×44 px (centred padding). Rightmost element.
Ham → X animationCross-fade with rotation. Three bars: top translateY +6 + rotate 45°, middle opacity 1→0, bottom translateY −6 + rotate −45°. Synchronous, duration-default (220 ms) · ease-in-out. Reverse identically on close.
CTA in mobile barYes — compact variant. Label "Cotizá" (Cotizá ahora is too long for 320 px viewports). Variant btn-primary-sm (32 px tall, padding 7+12, label-m). Sits to the left of the hamburger with space-3 (12 px) gap. Justification: cotizar is the dominant intent — burying it inside the drawer costs measurable conversion.
LayoutLogo left · spacer · CTA · gap-3 · Hamburger. Outer padding space-5 (20 px) horizontal.
Z-indexz-50 · 50. Same stack as desktop nav.
ResponsiveVisible 0–767 px. At ≥ md swap to §1.1 Top Navigation Bar.
AccessibilityHamburger is a <button> with aria-controls="primary-drawer" and aria-expanded reflecting state. aria-label="Abrir menú" (closed) / "Cerrar menú" (open).
DoTruncate CTA label to "Cotizá" on mobile. Reads as imperative voseo; the "ahora" is implied by its persistence.
Don'tDon't hide the CTA inside the drawer. Two taps to a quote is one tap too many.
